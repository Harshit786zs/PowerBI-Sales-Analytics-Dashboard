 # Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Business Problem
A retail company needs an interactive dashboard to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and top-performing products.

## Objectives
• Monitor Sales
• Analyze Profit
• Identify Best Categories
• Analyze Regions
• Track Customer Segments

## KPIs
• Total Sales
• Total Profit
• Total Orders
• Profit Margin

## Dashboard Features
• Dynamic Title
• Interactive Slicers
• Reset Filters Button
• Top 10 Products
• Business Insights
• DAX Measures

## Tools Used
• Power BI
• DAX
• Microsoft Excel

## Key Insights
• Technology generated the highest sales.
• Technology generated the highest profit.
• West region had the highest sales.
• Consumer is the largest customer segment.
• Overall Profit Margin is 12.56%.
