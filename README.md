 # 📊 Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, regional trends, and product performance through dynamic visualizations and KPI-driven insights.

# 🎯 Business Problem

Retail companies require an interactive reporting system to monitor overall business performance, identify profitable products and categories, analyze regional sales trends, and understand customer purchasing behavior.

Traditional reports often lack interactivity and make it difficult for managers to quickly identify opportunities and challenges.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by presenting key performance indicators and interactive visualizations in a single executive dashboard.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance
* Analyze profitability across categories
* Identify top-performing products
* Compare sales across regions
* Analyze customer segments
* Enable interactive business reporting
* Support data-driven decision making

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 💰 Total Sales
* 📈 Total Profit
* 🛒 Total Orders
* 📊 Profit Margin
